The Science of Insights

Understanding Saju and Manse-ryeok

Saju, also known as the Four Pillars of Destiny, is a traditional Eastern metaphysical system that analyzes a person's life based on their birth year, month, day, and hour. Each pillar represents a different aspect of life, including ancestors, parents, self, and children. By calculating the alignment of the five elements (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water), we can gain insights into potential life paths and personality traits.

The Value of Psychological Testing

Modern psychology often uses projective tests to understand a person's subconscious. By presenting ambiguous stimuli, like an image of a path or a forest, and asking for a preference, we can tap into the values and fears that guide your decision-making process.
